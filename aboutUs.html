About Us

SPARK is a non-profit organization built by students.

We plan to promote students' original designs in schools and make these original designs into products for sale on and off campus. After deducting the cost, all the income from the sales will be donated to ChenXingZhiJia, an organization helping disabled children.


Our Philosophy: "Everyone can shine."

We hope that SPARK can influence all students and that they can find their values and strengths.
